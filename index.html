When does the event start?
The event will start Friday, February 11th.
Doors will open at 6PM, and jamming will begin at 8PM.
How long is the event?
48 hours of jamming!
Where does the event take place?
Click here to see the map.
The address is 3700 Willingdon Ave, Burnaby, BC V5G 3H2.
Is this event only available to students?
No. Anyone is welcome.
How much does it cost?
$10 for students, and $20(?) for regular tickets.
We will ask for student IDs from student ticket holders.
Will I be reimbursed for my trip there?
Unfortunately, we will not be able to provide reimbursement for your trip.
Will meals be provided?
Five delicious meals will be provided by us and our sponsors. We will be providing dinner on Friday and Saturday, Breakfast on Saturday and Sunday, and lunch on Sunday.
Can I stay overnight?
Yes! However, please make sure you are properly equipped for the night. Warm clothes and maybe even a sleeping bag is recommended!
What should I bring with me?
Click here to see a list of items that we recommend you bring with you.
Do I need a team?
Jams are usually done is teams, but it's completely up to you whether or not you'd like to work in one. If you do want a team but do not have one, you will have a chance at the event before the jamming starts to mingle with the rest of attendees and make or join a team.
How much game development experience do I need to attend the event?
You do not need any experience at all. This is meant to be a fun event for anyone with any level of experience in game development. Even if you know very little about game development, you will learn a lot about it during the 48 hours.
